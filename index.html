WhatsApp-Message-Sender

WhatsApp Message Sender allows users to send messages without the need to save the recipient's phone number in their contacts. This means you can instantly compose and send messages to anyone, even if their contact information isn't stored on your device.

Additionally, the app enables businesses to create a clickable link that connects customers directly to their WhatsApp messaging service. This convenient feature streamlines customer communication and provides an efficient way for customers to get in touch with just a single click.
