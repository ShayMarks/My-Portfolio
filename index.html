About Me

Hey! I'm Shay Marks. I'm a third-year student studying software engineering at the College of Management. I enjoy delving into experiences and discovering ideas, researching new technologies, artificial intelligence tools, creating new things, delving into Full-Stack development, with an emphasis on Front-End development. I find enjoyment in the process, observing the results, and creating user-friendly designs that stimulate interaction.

Since I was a child, I loved to explore and discover new things on the computer. From new technologies to questions, I asked myself how everything works in the software, websites, games I came across, and that's where I started researching to find the answers to the questions.

I enjoy delving into programming languages and tools on my own and like to learn independently. I believe that when there is passion, it will be easy to persist in independent learning while enjoying the journey. On this site, I showcase my work portfolio projects that I created in order to solve problems, have fun, and learn. During the creation of each project, I improved my skills, faced challenges, bugs, and difficulties, and learned new things.

Apart from coding, I am an avid music lover and also create my own music and use AI tools to produce tracks that I share on my YouTube channel. In addition, I enjoy playing chess, like to swim, and play online games.

I believe that hard work and perseverance can help achieve any goal, so I'm always looking for new ways to improve and grow. This site is my personal portfolio, showing my journey and the projects I've worked on.

If you are looking for a developer with passion, dedicated and creative - I am here to help. You are welcome to browse my projects and contact me!
